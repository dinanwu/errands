# Errands

Errands is a game of karma and revenge heheh

You're just trying to get toilet paper from the grocery store. But the city is full of people being awful — honking at old ladies, yanking their dogs, double-parking in the bike lane, spitting on the sidewalk. You know the type.

Do good deeds along the way (pick up litter, help someone cross the street, catch a runaway puppy) and earn Karma Points. Then spend that karma on Looney Tunes-style revenge: drop an anvil on a car that bullied a pedestrian, launch a milkshake at someone who yanked their dog.

You are always the Righteous Avenger. Pure catharsis, no guilt.

## Tech

- Built with Godot 4 (GDScript)
- Isometric pixel art
- Browser-based (HTML5 export)

## Status

Work in progress. Currently in Phase 1 (core movement and street rendering).
